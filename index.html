Communications Arts Technology Program

The Communication Arts Technology Program at Jefferson Community & Technical College offers an Associate in Applied Science degree with three related tracks: Advertising Design, Commercial Photography, and Webpage Design, as well as certificates in Digital Media and Digital Video. Students are guided by real-world professionals working in the design, photography, and web design fields.
